Popular Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a question regarding our service?

What is TradCouples, and how does it differ from other dating sites?

TradCouples is a values-based matchmaking platform created for singles who prioritise faith, family, and long-term commitment. There is no endless scrolling or casual dating culture. Instead, we use an intentional, curated approach designed to help you find someone who shares your beliefs and relationship goals. Only men looking for women and women looking for men are accepted. No hookup culture.

Who is TradCouples for?

TradCouples is for singles who want a relationship built on traditional frameworks — where commitment, faith, and shared values form the foundation. Many members prefer clear relationship roles, long-term goals, and a family-centred lifestyle.

How does TradCouples maintain a safe and respectful environment?

We take safety seriously. Through identity verification, community guidelines, and active moderation, we ensure TradCouples remains a trustworthy, respectful space for like-minded individuals pursuing meaningful relationships.

How do I sign up?

Click “Start Your Journey” on the homepage, create your profile, and complete our verification process. Once approved, you can browse profiles and begin connecting with compatible members.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes. We offer tiered membership options with increasing levels of access and features. This helps maintain a high-quality community and ensures that matches are serious and intentional. See our pricing section for details.

Do I have to be religious to join?

While many of our members are people of faith, it is not a requirement. The core expectation is a commitment to traditional values, family-centred living, and purposeful relationships.

How do I create a profile?

Sign up for free and complete your profile details to reflect your values, lifestyle, and relationship goals. The more accurate your profile, the better your match results.

How does the matchmaking process work?

After registration, you will complete a detailed profile about your values, long-term goals, and lifestyle preferences. Our algorithm — combined with human review — suggests members who align with your beliefs and relationship intentions.

How do I find the right match?

Our system highlights profiles based on your compatibility preferences. You can then explore, communicate, and build connections with people who resonate with your lifestyle and values.

Can I set specific preferences for my matches?

Yes. You can personalise your preferences across age, location, faith alignment, lifestyle values, family goals, and relationship priorities.
